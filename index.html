03. Phenomenon Deep Dives

🎯
Asymmetry Analysis

Cluster 1: Unilateral Right Asymmetry

A deep dive investigation into the rare asymmetric phenotype (N=12 in main cluster + 18 outliers = 30 total). Explores clinical relevance, right-ear asymmetry patterns (Shooter's Ear), demographic indicators, and its mathematical consistency across survey cycles.

Read Deep Dive →
⚠️
Outlier Analysis

Outlier 18: Severe High-Frequency Loss

Detailed profile of a highly specific outlier group showing a steep sloping loss from normal thresholds at low frequencies to severe/profound loss at high frequencies. Includes correlation with reported tinnitus and occupational exposure.
